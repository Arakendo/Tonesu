# Phonosemantic Map

## Status: Draft — design reference

This file maps the full CV primitive space as a **family tree of meaning**. Each consonant defines a *family identity* — a broad semantic domain. Each vowel modulates meaning within that family, shifting register, granularity, or relational stance.

Inspired by Arabic trilateral root structure, where consonant combinations carry semantic families and vowel patterns carry grammatical/relational roles — but adapted for Tonesu's CV architecture where each primitive is a single consonant–vowel pair.

---

## How to read this map

```
Consonant = family identity (the deep semantic domain)
Vowel     = relational modulation within the family

     a          e          i          o          u
  abstract   emergent   precise   collective   interior
 /universal /forming    /signal  /distributed  /tacit
```

The five vowels are not random — they correspond to the V-prefix scope modifiers (spec/word-formation.md § V-Prefix Scope Modifiers):

| Vowel | Scope | Family inflection |
|-------|-------|------------------|
| `a`   | abstract / universal | the family concept at broadest scope |
| `e`   | emergent / forming | the family concept in transition or early state |
| `i`   | particular / signal-precise | the family concept as discrete instance or signal |
| `o`   | collective / distributed | the family concept as group property |
| `u`   | interior / foundational | the family concept in its tacit, deep, or substrate form |

This means every CV root can be read as: **{family-in-register}** — the consonant is the ontological family; the vowel is the register of encounter.

## The Families

### K — Containment · Boundary · Interior

`k` carries the deep sense of *bounded space* — something enclosed, held, or categorically limited.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ka` | ✅ primitive | deliberate action / will-in-act | intentional enclosure of the world's change — the act of *containing* causality in a directed form |
| `ke` | ✅ particle | pivot / counter-claim | discourse-boundary — the enclosed move that overturns the prior frame |
| `ki` | ✅ primitive | motion | movement *within* a bounded trajectory; the transition from one contained location to another |
| `ko` | ✅ primitive | containment / interior | the family center — enclosed space; what is *inside* |

**Family character:** `k` is the consonant of *enclosure and threshold*. `ko` is the archetype; `ki` is what crosses thresholds; `ka` is the act of deliberately meeting a boundary; `ke` is the discourse-edge that redirects.

**Open slots:** `ku` — potential: tacit/foundational containment (deep interior); `ke` taken by particle.

---

### T — Pattern · Time · Thought

`t` carries the deep sense of *sequence and pattern* — things that have structure across time or in abstraction.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ti` | ✅ primitive | time / sequence | pattern-as-temporal-instance; the moment in the flow |
| `to` | ✅ primitive | conceptual pattern / thought | pattern at its most abstract — the model, the idea |

**Family character:** `t` is the consonant of *pattern recognition and temporal structure*. `to` = pattern as thought (abstract axis); `ti` = pattern as time (temporal axis). Together they cover the two fundamental ways pattern presents itself: as an idea in a mind, or as a sequence in the world.

**Open slots:** `ta`, `te`, `tu` — potential: universal pattern (law / principle), emergent pattern (forming idea), interior pattern (tacit knowledge/intuition).

---

### S — Signal · Structure · Sound

`s` carries the deep sense of *form that carries information* — shape, signal, encoded representation, organized perception.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `se` | ✅ primitive | perception / sense | the emergent signal — raw, forming, unprocessed |
| `si` | ✅ primitive | signal / representation | the discrete encoded signal — precise, particular |
| `so` | ✅ primitive | sound / acoustic signal | the collective signal domain — the acoustic field |
| `su` | ✅ primitive | structure / order | the universal form — arrangement at broadest scope |

**Family character:** `s` is the consonant of *form and signal*. The vowel progression is nearly complete and maps cleanly:
- `su` = form as universal/abstract structure
- `se` = form in emergent perception
- `si` = form as precise encoded signal
- `so` = form as collective acoustic field

**Open slot:** `sa` — potential: abstract/universal signal (the signal-in-general; "what can be signaled").

---

### N — Relation · Negation · Number

`n` is a bifurcated family — it holds both *connection* (`ne`, `nu`) and *absence/negation* (`no`). This tension is productive: the same consonant family governs both bond and its dissolution.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ne` | ✅ primitive | relation / connection | bond as universal abstract — the link itself |
| `no` | ✅ primitive | negation / absence | the collective absence — the slot where connection is not |
| `nu` | ✅ primitive | quantity / number | the precise counting dimension — how many of the connected |

**Family character:** `n` is the consonant of *connection and its measure*. `ne` = a bond exists; `no` = no bond; `nu` = how many bonds / how much. Philosophically: you cannot count what is not connected, and absence is the limit case of connection.

**Open slots:** `na` — taken by the naming prefix (`na-X` = named individual); `ni` — potential: a particular/specific bond; `nu` — taken.

### P — Part · Place · Plural

`p` carries the deep sense of *parthood and spatial membership* — being a component of something, occupying a portion.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `pa` | ✅ primitive | place / space | location as the abstract dimension of parthood — where something is |
| `pe` | ✅ primitive | part / component | the emergent part — a piece in the context of a whole |
| `pu` | ✅ primitive | plurality / collective | interior-distributed parthood — the many as a group |

**Family character:** `p` is the consonant of *spatial and partitive membership*. `pe` = one part; `pu` = many parts; `pa` = the space in which parts reside.

**Open slots:** `pi` — potential: a particular/specific part (the identified member); `po` — potential: the collective place (territory).

### M — Matter · Measure · Mind

`m` carries the deep sense of *substrate and medium* — what things are made of, the base upon which everything else stands.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ma` | ✅ primitive | matter / substance | abstract substrate — the universal material ground |
| `mu` | ✅ primitive | object / artifact | interior matter — matter organized into intentional form |
| `mi` | reserved | first-person pronoun | the particular/precise self — the singular point of interiority |

**Family character:** `m` is the consonant of *material substrate and the self*. `ma` = matter as universal ground; `mu` = matter as shaped artifact; `mi` = the first-person singular (the ultimate interior matter — the self).

**Open slots:** `me` — potential: emergent matter (forming material; matter in transition); `mo` — potential: collective matter (the material world as whole).

---

### F — Force · Boundary · Affect

`f` carries the deep sense of *limit and felt experience* — what pushes against a surface, what marks an edge, and what is felt from within.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `fa` | ✅ primitive | affective substrate | interior felt state — the `u`-register of force: what force *feels like from inside* |
| `fe` | ✅ primitive | boundary / limit | the emergent edge — the threshold where one thing ends and another begins |

**Family character:** `f` is the consonant of *edge and interior experience*. `fe` = the limit as perceived/emergent boundary. `fa` = the inward pressure of that limit on the self. Together they cover the phenomenology of constraint: the edge exists (`fe`); the organism feels the edge (`fa`).

**Design note:** `fa` initially seems to belong with emotion (`fa` as affect). But read through the family lens: `fa` is force *interior to the organism* — the `u`-register (interior/foundational) of the body's dynamic state. It is not "feeling" as concept; it is the *medium* through which the organism registers impingement.

**Open slots:** `fi`, `fo`, `fu` — relatively open; high potential for future primitives or V-prefix compounds.

---

### R — Recurrence · Energy · Coherence

`r` carries the deep sense of *cycle and dynamic return* — energy that flows, patterns that repeat, systems that cohere.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ra` | ✅ primitive | energy / force | abstract energy — the universal capacity to cause change |
| `re` | ✅ primitive | repetition / cycle | emergent recurrence — the pattern that returns |
| `ru` | ✅ primitive | unity / singularity | interior coherence — the whole held together from within |

**Family character:** `r` is the consonant of *dynamic coherence*. `ra` = the energy that drives; `re` = the pattern that recurs; `ru` = the unity that holds. Together: the three modes of dynamic persistence — force, cycle, and integrity.

**Open slots:** `ri`, `ro` — potential: particular energy instance (a specific charge/impulse); collective coherence (unity as group property).

---

### L — Life · Light · Social Agent

`l` carries the deep sense of *animacy and visibility* — what is alive, what can be seen, what acts as an agent in the social world.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `la` | reserved | agent marker prefix | the abstract social-agent role — the universal subject position |
| `li` | ✅ primitive | social agent / person | particular social agent — the precise person |
| `lo` | reserved | patient marker prefix | collective/recipient position — the object of action |
| `lu` | ✅ primitive | light / visibility | interior light — illumination as the foundational condition of visual experience |

**Family character:** `l` is the consonant of *agency and visibility*. `li` = the person as precise social agent; `lu` = the light that makes persons visible to each other; `la` / `lo` = the grammar roles that structure how agents and patients relate. The family bridges physical visibility and social recognition.

**Open slots:** `le` — potential: emergent agency (the forming agent; becoming-person).

### D — Direction · Decay · Result

`d` carries the deep sense of *directional flow toward an endpoint* — movement toward, decline, or production.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `de` | ✅ primitive | decay / decrease | emergent endpoint — the thing moving toward its end |
| `di` | ✅ primitive | direction | particular/precise vector — the exact trajectory |
| `du` | ✅ primitive | result / effect | interior output — the thing that emerges from a process |

**Family character:** `d` is the consonant of *directed outcome*. `di` = the direction of travel; `de` = the travel toward diminishment; `du` = what arrives at the end. The family traces a single arc: direction → endpoint → product.

**Open slots:** `da`, `do` — potential: abstract direction (orientation-in-general); collective result (shared outcome).

---

### B — Growth · Emergence · Life-stage

`b` carries the deep sense of *upward generation* — increase, building, new life, origin of things.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `be` | ✅ primitive | growth / increase | emergent growth — the forming, building, rising state |

**Family character:** `b` is the consonant of *generation and increase*. Currently sparse — `be` is the founding member. The family suggests: `ba` = abstract growth (growth-as-principle); `bi` = particular growth event; `bo` = collective growth (proliferation); `bu` = interior growth (latent potential).

**Note:** `b` and `d` are sibling consonants (voiced bilabial / voiced alveolar stop) representing the two poles of directed change: `b` upward; `d` downward. This is a structural phonosemantic pair.

### H — Heat · Breath · Voice

`h` carries the deep sense of *warm outflowing presence* — thermal state, breath, the outward signal of interior life.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ha` | ✅ primitive | heat / thermal state | abstract thermal quality — the universal warmth property of matter |
| `he` | ✅ particle | vocative | emergent outflowing address — the breath that reaches toward another |

**Family character:** `h` is the consonant of *warmth and breath*. `ha` = the internal thermal state of matter; `he` = the outward breath that calls. These are the two axes of warmth: inward (temperature) and outward (address).

**Open slots:** `hi`, `ho`, `hu` — relatively open; `ho` has high phonosemantic potential for collective warmth (community atmosphere).

### W — Will · Boundary-of-intention · Ethics

`w` carries the deep sense of *directed will and constraint* — what agents intend, what they are permitted, what they are bound to.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `wi` | ✅ primitive | will / intention | the particular act of willing — the precise directed desire |
| `wo` | reserved | second-person pronoun | the collective other — the addressed plural you |

**Family character:** `w` is the consonant of *will and address*. `wi` = the directed act of intending; `wo` = the collective interlocutor (you/plural). Together they cover the dyad of intentional communication: the will of the speaker and the attention of the listener.

**Open slots:** `wa`, `we`, `wu` — `wa` has high potential as an abstract will-principle (ethics / normativity in general); `we` as emergent intention (forming desire).

---

### V — Value · Voice · Exchange

`v` carries the deep sense of *worth and expression* — what has quality, what is voiced, what is given out.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `vo` | ✅ primitive | value / quality | collective value — quality as a distributed property |
| `vu` | reserved | second-person singular pronoun | interior particular other — the foundational addressee |

**Family character:** `v` is the consonant of *expressed worth*. `vo` = the quality or worth of things; `vu` = the you that receives it. Together: what is valued, and for whom.

**Open slots:** `va`, `ve`, `vi` — `va` as abstract value principle; `ve` as emergent worth (forming/growing quality).

### Y — Attention · Orientation · Addressal

`y` carries the deep sense of *attentive orientation* — turning toward, marking salience, directing focus.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ya` | ✅ particle | attention signal | abstract/universal attention — the broadest pragmatic pivot: attend to this |
| `yu` | reserved | group pronoun (we/us) | interior group — the collective interior: us |

**Family character:** `y` is the consonant of *orientation and togetherness*. `ya` = attend (orient to this); `yu` = we/us (the interior group that shares orientation). Philosophically: attention is what turns a self toward others; `yu` is the result of sustained shared attention.

**Open slots:** `ye`, `yi`, `yo` — `ye` has potential as emergent attention (forming awareness / semi-attentive state).

---

### Z — Living organism · Perception · Mutual exchange

`z` carries the deep sense of *animate interaction* — the living thing perceiving and the event of mutual engagement.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `ze` | reserved | anaphoric pronoun (it/that) | emergent reference — the thing just named, now carried forward |
| `zi` | ✅ primitive | mutual / coupling event | particular coupling — the precise bilateral exchange |
| `zo` | ✅ primitive | living thing | collective life — organisms as the distributed living world |

**Family character:** `z` is the consonant of *living interaction*. `zo` = organisms (life distributed across the world); `zi` = the event of two organisms coupling; `ze` = the rhetorical act of pointing to the living thing just named. The family traces: life exists (`zo`) → life interacts (`zi`) → life is referred to (`ze`).

**Open slots:** `za`, `zu` — `za` as abstract life-principle (vitality / animation as such); `zu` as interior life (the foundational substrate of the living: what makes it alive from within).

### G — Cause · Growth-origin · Grammar

`g` carries the deep sense of *origination* — what gives rise, what starts the chain, what generates.

| Root | Status | Gloss | Family reading |
|------|--------|-------|----------------|
| `go` | ✅ primitive | cause / origin | the collective/distributed cause — the origin point of causal chains |
| `ge` | reserved | derivational suffix (-ge = quality/property) | emergent quality — what arises out of something; the property that grows from the base |

**Family character:** `g` is the consonant of *origination and emergence*. `go` = the causal origin itself; `ge` = what emerges from origins as a stable property. Together: cause and its product.

**Open slots:** `ga`, `gi`, `gu` — `ga` as abstract origin principle; `gi` as a particular causal chain instance.

## Phonosemantic Pairs and Triads

Structurally related families that form semantic clusters:

### The Change Pair: `b` / `d`
`b` = directed increase · `d` = directed decrease. Sibling voiced stops; semantic mirror pair.
```
be   growth / increase
de   decay / decrease
```
Both are processes; both are directed. The consonant pair encodes the axis of directed change.

### The Perception Triad: `s` / `f` / `z`
Signal, boundary-felt, animate-interaction. The three faces of how organism meets world:
```
se / si / so / su   the objective signal (world → organism)
fa / fe             the boundary's interior felt-pressure (limit → organism)
zie / zi / zo       the animate life doing the perceiving
```

### The Pattern Pair: `t` / `r`
Thought / cycle. Abstract pattern in mind vs recurring pattern in world:
```
to   conceptual pattern
ti   temporal sequence
ra   energetic cycle
re   recurrence / rhythm
```

### The Social Triad: `l` / `w` / `v`
Agency / will / value. The three primitives of social life:
```
li   the social agent
wi   the agent's will
vo   the worth being pursued
```

### The Space–Part Pair: `k` / `p`
Containment / parthood. How things are bounded and how they belong:
```
ko   interior / contained
pa   location / place
pe   part / member
pu   plural / collective
```

### The Causation Pair: `g` / `n`
Origination / connection. The two ways things relate:
```
go   causal origin
du   causal result
ne   relational bond
no   relational absence
```

## Open Questions

- [ ] Should the vowel-family claim (consonant defines family, vowel defines register) be formally adopted as a design rule? Currently emergent but not normative.
- [ ] `b` family is sparse (only `be`). Does `ba` warrant admission as an abstract growth principle? Not until corpus produces pressure.
- [ ] `f` family open slots (`fi`, `fo`, `fu`) — `fu` has potential as "tacit/interior boundary" (the deep structure of limits). Watch for corpus pressure.
- [ ] `t` family open slots — `ta` (abstract pattern / law / principle) is the most tempting. Resist until the corpus requires it.
- [ ] The consonant-family hypothesis predicts that new primitives, if ever admitted, should slot into an existing family's open vowel slots rather than introducing phonosemantic orphans. This is a design discipline implication, not yet a rule.
