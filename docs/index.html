Tonesu

A constructed language built on generative roots.

Tonesu is a constructed language designed around a small set of primitive roots that combine compositionally to express any concept. Words are not looked up — they are built.

A speaker who knows the ~34 primitive roots and the compound order rule can construct and interpret unfamiliar words without a dictionary. For example: toli (to-li) = knowledge-agent = scholar; tonofe (to-no-fe) = knowledge without boundary = omniscience.
