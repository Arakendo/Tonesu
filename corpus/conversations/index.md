# Conversations — Index

Multi-turn dialogues in Tonesu. Each conversation is a complete exchange, annotated turn by turn. Conversations are longer and more contextually dense than single-sentence tests; they are the primary validation ground for discourse-level constructs (epistemic testimony, question formation, evidentiality, nested attribution).

## Conversation vs Sentence

A conversation entry is used when:
- The test requires more than one turn to set up
- The critical construction appears in the reply, not the prompt
- The construct depends on prior discourse context (pronoun resolution, `ze` back-reference, floor-state tracking)

Single sentences that happen to be dialogue lines go in the sentence files (S-series) with a note about their conversational context.
