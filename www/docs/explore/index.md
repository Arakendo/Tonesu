# Explore

This section shows Tonesu as a generative system — not just rules, but the language working, building, and exploring its own space.

---

## [Patterns](patterns.md)

The productive templates that generate whole families of compounds. Learn a pattern and you can build dozens of words you've never seen before — and read them correctly on first encounter.

---

## [Sandbox](sandbox.md)

Exploratory assemblies. What happens when you combine these roots? Novel compounds that may not be registered yet, constructed to probe what the language can express. Some will be useful; some will reveal gaps; all are valid.

---

## [Worked Examples](compounds.md)

Specific concepts traced from idea to compound. Which roots were chosen, why that structure, where the parse lands, what alternatives were considered.

---

## [Sentences](sentences.md)

Tonesu sentences drawn from the corpus with full breakdowns. Starts simple and builds up to complex grammar, nested structures, and multi-clause reasoning.
