# Corpus

The canonical body of attested Tonesu sentences. Every entry is numbered, parsed, and annotated to show the grammar working in practice — not rules in isolation, but language doing real things.

Sentences are drawn from stress-test batches covering grammar edge cases, theological register, numerals, biological naming, and everyday usage.
