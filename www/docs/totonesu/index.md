# Totonesu

*to-tonesu* — knowledge of Tonesu in use. This section is the language working: constructions under pressure, conversations with consequences, and the places where the design breaks.

Everything here is drawn from the corpus or built as a deliberate probe. The goal isn't to show that Tonesu is elegant — it's to show whether it *works*.
