# Stage 4 — Derived vocabulary

Work through the derived registry by domain or through corpus sentences. For each
entry: parse it from its roots, verify that the gloss makes sense from the composition.
Corpus sentences are the main exposure vehicle — real usage, not contrived examples.

Ambiguity drills continue here with longer compounds.

After this stage you should have a working vocabulary of 50–100 derived forms and be
able to look up any unfamiliar entry and parse it independently.
