# Stage 1 — Roots in context

Roots are introduced through sentences, in clusters of 3–6 per lesson. Reuse is heavy.
After 4–5 lessons, you should know ~20 roots from repeated exposure — not from a list,
but from having encountered them in use.

The [primitive inventory](../tonesu/primitives.md) exists as reference — consult it
when a root comes up, not as a study target.
