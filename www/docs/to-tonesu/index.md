# To'tonesu

*to'tonesu* — knowledge standing apart from Tonesu, looking at it. This section is the theory: why the language behaves the way it does, where its structures come from, and what they reveal about the design space.

The juncture `'` in the name marks deliberate analytic distance — the same way it marks a pre-bound unit inside a compound, creating a seam between the observer and the observed.
