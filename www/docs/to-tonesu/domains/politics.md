# Politics

*Vocabulary for power, authority, governance, legitimacy, and collective decision-making in Tonesu.*

Politics is where sociology meets deontic structure: who holds authority, how it is conferred and contested, how collective decisions are made and enforced. The DEB-001 (debate) and DIP-001 (diplomacy) corpus batches are the primary stress-tests. Key roots: `ra` (force/power), `li` (person/agent), `vo` (value/legitimacy), `re` (structure/canon), `o-` prefix (collective scope).

Open questions: how does Tonesu distinguish authority conferred by role from authority conferred by force? What carries the weight of `sovereignty`?
