# Theology & Religion

*Vocabulary for the divine, the sacred, and the institutional — God's attributes, creation, transcendence, ritual, canon, worship, and the embodied forms of religious life.*

In Tonesu the abstract and the practiced share roots: the same `si` (signal/prayer), `vo` (value/goodness), `pa` (presence), and `re` (structure) that ground theological predication also generate liturgy, community, and canon. The `[X]-no-fe` extremal suffix (THO-001) anchors the attribute vocabulary: `tonofe` (omniscience), `ranofe` (omnipotence), `vonofe` (omnibenevolence), `panofe` (omnipresence), `gonofe` (necessary being), `tinofe` (eternality). Key corpus batches: THO-*, GOD-*, T-REL-*, T-WIT-*, EXO, MAT, JOH, LSP.
