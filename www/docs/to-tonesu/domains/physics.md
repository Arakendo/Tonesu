# Physics

*Physical vocabulary in Tonesu — energy, force, matter, space, time, motion, and the laws governing them.*

`ra` (force/energy) is the core primitive. The PMS-001 (conservation), T-AX-* (causation), and GEO-001 (spatial) corpus batches stress-test this domain. CVCC exceptional anchors cover physical constants. This section explores how Tonesu renders physical law, measurement, and theoretical structure.
