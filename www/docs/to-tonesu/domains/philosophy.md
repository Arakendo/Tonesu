# Philosophy

*Philosophical vocabulary in Tonesu — being, knowledge, identity, causation, necessity, mind, and the structure of philosophical argument.*

Tonesu is itself a philosophical project: its root ontology encodes a theory of what exists and how it relates. This section surfaces that implicit ontology explicitly — tracing the primitives back to first principles, exploring what the language presupposes, and stress-testing it against canonical philosophical problems.

Key threads: identity (`ne` / `helm` / `helms` three-way distinction), necessity (`gonofe`), epistemics (`to`, `fa`, evidential frames), causation (`go` vs `;` — Hume's distinction).
