# Sociology

*Social vocabulary in Tonesu — collective action, group identity, institution, role, norm, conflict, and the structures that emerge from aggregated persons.*

The `o-` (collective scope-modifier) prefix is a central tool here. This section explores how Tonesu handles the gap between individual `li` (person) and aggregate social structures — guilds, communities, states, movements — and the vocabulary of social dynamics.
