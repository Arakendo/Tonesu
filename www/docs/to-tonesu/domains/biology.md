# Biology

*Exploration of biological vocabulary in Tonesu — living systems, anatomy, ecology, growth, reproduction, and the boundary between organism and environment.*

The kind-term taxonomy in `registry/kinds.md` provides the foundational tier. This section traces how Tonesu's root system handles biological structure: what `zo` (life-signal), `be` (growth/decay), `pa` (presence/place), and `su` (structure) generate across the domain, and where compound forms become necessary.
