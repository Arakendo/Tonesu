# Ethics

*Ethical vocabulary in Tonesu — value, obligation, harm, virtue, consent, responsibility, and the grounds for moral judgment.*

`vo` (value/goodness) is the axial root. `vonofe` (omnibenevolence) marks the upper bound; `mmp` and `no-vo` the lower. This section explores how Tonesu handles moral discourse: agent responsibility, the structure of obligations, harm, and the distinction between descriptive and normative claims.
