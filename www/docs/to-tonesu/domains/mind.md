# Mind & Psychology

*Vocabulary for consciousness, perception, intention, affect, memory, and the structure of mental life in Tonesu.*

Tonesu handles mind unusually: `fa` (affect/feeling) has explicit grammatical marking (the `fa-CON` corpus batch), and `si` (perception/signal) anchors both external sensing and inner attention. OPX-001 (self-perception) and IPX-001 (identity under change) stress-test the boundary of self. Key roots: `fa` (affect), `si` (signal/perceive), `to` (knowledge), `ki` (change/intention), `wi` (relation/will).

The `o-` (collective) vs `i-` (particular) scope modifiers create pressure: is emotional experience `a-fa` (universal affect-form) or `i-fa` (this specific feeling)? What does `fa-no-fe` mean?
