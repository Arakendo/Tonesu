# Law

*Legal vocabulary in Tonesu — obligation, permission, prohibition, evidence, judgment, procedure, and the structure of normative systems.*

Law is a domain of deontic structure: what is required, permitted, and forbidden; how evidence and argument produce verdicts; how institutions encode and enforce norms. Corpus batch LGL-001 provides the stress-test baseline.
