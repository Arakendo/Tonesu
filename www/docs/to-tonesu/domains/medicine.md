# Medicine

*Vocabulary for health, illness, body, and clinical intervention in Tonesu.*

Medicine sits at the intersection of biology (body-as-system), ethics (care obligations), and causation (diagnosis as causal inference). The MED-001 corpus batch is the baseline stress-test. Key roots: `zo` (life/signal), `be` (growth/decay), `pa` (presence/place — locus of symptom), `ra` (energy/force — intervention), `no-be` (pathological decay).

Interesting pressure points: Tonesu's evidential frame `()` is clinically natural — reported symptoms are epistemically distinct from observed signs. The `go` vs `;` distinction matters for diagnosis vs mere correlation.
