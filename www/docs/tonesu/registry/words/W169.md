## Definition

knowledge of truth; knowledge of Tonesu; the activity of knowing and using the language. Inherits the dual reading of to-ne-su (W000): to + tonesu = knowledge-of-[truth / the language]. As knowledge-of-truth it is participatory — truth known from within, through engagement rather than detached analysis. As knowledge-of-the-language it is the language studied through use and attestation.
