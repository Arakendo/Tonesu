## Composition

ko (container/enclosure) + fe (boundary/limit) + ki (change/transformation). fe-ki = limit-change/transformation = the act of altering limits. ko modifies fe-ki = the containing of that limit-transformation = protective magic.

## Notes

W180. First attested DND-001 (S600). The -ki suffix is shared by all eight school compounds (W180-W187), marking them all as change-arts. ko-fe patient in S600: lo-ko-fe = "containing-limit" as what is operated upon.
