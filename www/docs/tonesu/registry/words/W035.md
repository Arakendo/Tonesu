## Composition

de (decay/decrease) + be (growth/increase) — head-final: growth that is characterized as occurring after and in response to decay; recovery-growth; the specific form of increase that counteracts a prior decrease

## Definition

repair; restore; reverse decay; bring back to functional state

## Notes

The de-be compound is semantically precise: not all growth is repair (ka-be covers creation from nothing), and not all decay states are followed by growth.
de-be specifically encodes directional recovery: from-decay-toward-growth.
Applicable to physical objects (repairing a machine), infrastructure, relationships, or any system that can decay and be restored.
Contrast: ka-be (creation from scratch, S014); de (decay, no recovery, S026).

## Related

be (primitive: growth), de (primitive: decay), ka-be (create), ra-be (energy-growth)

## Examples

"la-ze  lo-ze  ka-de-be-past" — she repaired it (S027) "ka-de-be" = the act of repairing
