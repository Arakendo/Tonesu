## Composition

ki (motion) + ne (connection/bond) = motion-bond = what binds to the body to facilitate or mark movement.

## Definition

sandal; footwear; what is bonded to the foot to enable or mark motion. The motion-connector worn on the body for movement.

## Notes

The command to remove sandals ("take off your shoes, for you are standing on holy ground") forces this compound. ki-ne as footwear is a clean compositional reading: sandals are literally the motion-bond between foot and ground.
Removal command: ka-de-ne lo-ki-ne = deliberately-unbind the motion-bond = take off the sandals.

## Related
