## Composition

ki (motion) + pa (place) — a place whose defining character is directed passage.
Head-final: pa (place) is head; ki specifies the motion character.

## Definition

corridor; passage; a place defined by motion through it.

## Notes

Paired with ko-pa (W048, room). The distinction: a room exists to contain; a corridor exists to pass through. Extends to any transitive passage space: hallway, airlock approach, transit tube.

## Related
