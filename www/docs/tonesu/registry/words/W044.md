## Composition

ha (thermal state) + de (decay/decrease) — thermal decrease. Head-final: de (decrease) is the head; ha specifies thermal domain (vs ra-de = power loss).

## Definition

cooling; the process of thermal decrease; reduction of heat in a system.

## Notes

Systematic pair with ha-be (heating). ra-de ≠ ha-de: ra-de = energy decrease (power failure/deceleration); ha-de = heat reduction. Orthogonal properties.

## Related

ha (thermal state), de (decrease), ra-de (power loss — contrast), ne-fe (W042)

## Examples

"lo-mu  ne-fe  ha-de" — the machine needs cooling (S060)
