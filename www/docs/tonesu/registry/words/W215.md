## Composition

ma (matter) + ne (relation/bond) + ki (motion/change) = matter-[relational-motion] = the motion that brings matter into relation.

Right-branch parse: ma-[ne-ki] = matter-[connecting motion].

The three-root chain: matter (substrate) + relation (goal) + motion (mechanism).

## Definition

mix; combine; blend. The physical action of bringing matter into relational unity through motion. Covers: stirring, folding, blending, combining, kneading — any physical act whose purpose and result is the relational bonding of previously separate matter. The ne (relation/bond) root encodes that the goal is union, not mere motion.

## Notes
