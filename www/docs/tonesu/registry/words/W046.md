## Composition

ma (matter/substance) + no (negation) + de (decay/dissolution) — substance that negates decay. Head-final: de is head of no-de; ma specifies material class.

## Definition

medicine; treatment; a substance whose function is to preserve or restore biological integrity.

## Notes

Works for any substance preventing or reversing deterioration in a living system. mu-no-de (unregistered) = preservative coating on an artifact — same internal structure, different subject class (mu vs ma).

## Related

ma (matter), no (negation), de (decay), ne-fe (W042), zo-li (person/patient)

## Examples

"lo-zo-li  ta-ti-now  no-ne-fe  ma-no-de" — the patient no longer needs medicine (S063)
