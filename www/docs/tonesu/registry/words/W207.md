## Composition

to (conceptual pattern/thought) + ra (force/energy) + li (social agent). Head: li (agent-type). Modifier: to-ra (knowledge-force) = the agent for whom knowledge IS force. Right-branching: to-(ra-li) = knowledge-kind-of-force-person.

## Notes

W207. First attested DND-004 (S652). D&D Classes batch. Parallel compound structure to vo-ra-li (W209, cleric): both are X-ra-li where X is the source that the agent converts into force. Wizard: to (knowledge). Cleric: vo (sacred worth). This parallel makes the fighter/wizard/cleric triad structurally legible: ra-ka-li (direct force), to-ra-li (knowledge-sourced force), vo-ra-li (worth-sourced force). Contrast to-li (W003, scholar): a scholar holds knowledge; a wizard mobilizes knowledge as power. The ra root marks the distinction.
