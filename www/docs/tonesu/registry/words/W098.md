## Composition

si (signal/encoded-representation) + de (decay/past) — a signal that has passed into the record; the signal from an earlier time, now a fixed datum.
Distinct from bare si (active signal) and si-su (archive as institution).

## Definition

past signal; signal-of-record; historical signal data; prior observation data

## Notes

Three-attestation corpus basis: S106, S172, S174.
si-de = the specific past signal datum (one record); si-su (W028 type) = the archive as a whole system; bare si = active or current signal still in transit or not yet processed.

## Related

si (signal), de (decay/past), to-ko (W044, memory), si-su (archive)
