## Composition

to (knowledge/pattern) + su (structure)

## Definition

organized knowledge; system, model, framework, theory

## Notes

to-su = committed/established framework (working theory promoted to structured knowledge). The approximation operator gives a useful distinction:
~to-su = tentative/working theory (a knowledge structure that is acknowledged as provisional). Contrast: to-su asserts organized structure; ~to-su marks the organization itself as approximate. This distinction is available without any new vocabulary.

## Related

to-su-mu (device), to-su-li (expert)

## Examples

"the to-su explained the phenomenon"
