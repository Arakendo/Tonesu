## Composition

zo (living thing/organism) + re (repetition/cycle) + li (social agent). Head: li (agent-type). Modifier: zo-re (organism-cycle) = the cycles of living things. A zo-re-li is a person defined by their embeddedness in living cycles. Right-branching: zo-(re-li) = organism-kind-of-cycle-person.

## Notes

W210. First attested DND-004 (S655). D&D Classes batch. Distinct from zo-su-ka-li (W150, ecologist): an ecologist studies living systems structurally from an organized position; a druid is embedded within the cycles of living things. Study vs embeddedness. Also distinct from zo-ki-li (W212, ranger): a ranger moves through the living world; a druid IS within its cycles. Motion vs containment. The predicate in S655 (ko lo-re-zo) captures the containment: the druid does not study or traverse the life-cycle; they are inside it.
