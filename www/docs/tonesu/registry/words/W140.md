## Composition

lu (light) + ti (time/temporal sequence) = light-time = the time-
period defined by light. Head-final: ti (time) is head; lu specifies the defining quality of the temporal period — the kind of time characterized by light.

## Definition

daytime; the light-period; the temporal interval characterized by the presence of light; daylight hours. The named temporal phase defined by lu (light). Pair: no-lu-ti (W141: nighttime).

## Notes

lu-ti / no-lu-ti (W141) form a minimal pair covering the two alternating temporal phases of the daily cycle. Together they partition the day into light-period and dark-period.
