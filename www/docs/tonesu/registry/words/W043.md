## Composition

zo (living thing) + no (negation) + ki (motion) — living non-motion. Head-final:
ki (motion) is the head; no-ki = non-motion; zo specifies biological domain.

## Definition

rest; sleep; the state of reduced or absent voluntary motion in a living body.

## Notes

Distinct from mu-no-ki (device at rest / powered-off). zo prefix makes this specifically a living body's rest state, not mere mechanical stillness.

## Related
