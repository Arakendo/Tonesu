## Composition

vo (value/worth/quality) + ra (force/energy) + li (social agent). Head: li (agent-type). Modifier: vo-ra (worth-force) = one who applies force as an instrument of sacred worth. Right-branching: vo-(ra-li) = worth-kind-of-force-person. The vo (worth/sacred) specifies the source and orientation of the force, distinguishing from to-ra-li (W207, wizard: source = knowledge) and ra-ka-li (W206, fighter: source = direct volitional effort).

## Notes

W209. First attested DND-004 (S654). D&D Classes batch. Parallel to to-ra-li (W207, wizard) in construction: both are X-ra-li. The X-ra-li pattern captures the class trio fighter/wizard/cleric as: direct force (ra-ka-li), knowledge-sourced force (to-ra-li), worth-sourced force (vo-ra-li). Sentence S652 and S654 use the same predicate structure (ra-be go X) to make this parallel explicit. Note: wi-vo-li (W211, paladin) also invokes vo but is wi-vo rather than vo-ra — the paladin's primary characteristic is the sworn will (wi), with vo as the target of that will, not the source of force.
