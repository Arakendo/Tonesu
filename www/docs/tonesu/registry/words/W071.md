## Composition

ra (energy/force) + vo (value/quality) — the quality dimension of energy.
Head-final: vo (quality) is head; ra specifies the domain.

## Definition

signal intensity; energy quality; the qualitative and quantitative character of an energetic or signal output.

## Notes

Third member of the [domain]-vo quality pattern: ha-vo (thermal quality, W033-domain), pa-nu (spatial quantity uses nu not vo), ra-vo (energy/signal quality). ra-vo can describe both raw energy output (power level of an engine) and signal strength (signal quality of a communication channel). In Tonesu:
a pa-ra (field, W053) has a ra-vo; an anomalous emission that breaks a si-fe (W070, signal threshold) has a measurable ra-vo.

## Related

ra (energy), vo (value/quality), si-fe (W070), pa-ra (W053, field)
