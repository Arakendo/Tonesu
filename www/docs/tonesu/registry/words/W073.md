## Composition

wi-to (plan, W045) + su (structure/organization) — the organized, structured form of a plan. Head-final: su (structure) is head; wi-to specifies the content domain as purposive-conceptual.

## Definition

design specification; a formally organized purposive artifact; a plan that has been systematized into a structure that can be collectively reviewed, revised, and published.

## Notes

Parallel to the to / to-su upgrade: to (knowledge/concept) → to-su (organized body of knowledge). wi-to (plan/concept) → wi-to-su (plan organized as a formal structure). The su head marks the upgrade from mental/paper artifact to institutionally organized structure — something reviewable, publishable, subject to to-fe-li scrutiny. A plan that has not gone through formalization is wi-to; once it has been systematized as a team document or formal spec, it is wi-to-su.
Distinct from wi-ka-su (W054, ritual): wi-ka-su is an organized system of deliberate actions (the doing); wi-to-su is an organized intentional-concept structure (the specification).
