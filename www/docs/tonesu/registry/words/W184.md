## Composition

ra (force/energy) + be (emergence) + ki (change/transformation). be-ki = manifestation-change = the art of altering or directing emergence. ra modifies be-ki = force-directed manifestation = evocation. The parse ra-(be-ki) is right-branching and unambiguous.

## Notes
