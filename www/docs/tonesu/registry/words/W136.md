## Definition

lust; the affective desire for coupling; the affect state in which will is oriented toward a mutual-coupling event with another person as its direct object. A specific sub-type of fa-wi (affect-will = desire) directed toward zi (mutual coupling). Contrasted with fa-wi-de (W127: temptation = affect-will toward harm in general).
Greek epithumia (desire/lust) as used in Matthew 5:28.
