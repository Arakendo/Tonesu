## Composition

zo (living organism) + de (decay/decrease) — biological decay from use.
Head-final: de (decay) is head; zo specifies the domain as living/biological.

## Definition

fatigue; physical tiredness; the wearing-down of a living body through exertion.

## Notes

Third member of the de-domain contrast set: ra-de (power/energy loss = power failure or deceleration), ha-de (W044, thermal decrease = cooling), zo-de (biological wear = fatigue). The three are strictly orthogonal: a person who is zo-de is not necessarily ra-de or ha-de; an overheating engine is ha-de, not zo-de.
The de head with a domain modifier has proven productive across four roots now (ra-de, ha-de, zo-de, no-de = preservation).

## Related
