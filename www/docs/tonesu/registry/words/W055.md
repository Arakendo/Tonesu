## Composition

fe (boundary/limit) + su (structure/organization) — a boundary that has been organized into a structure. Head-final: su (structure) is head; fe specifies the boundary function.

## Definition

ward; protective barrier; a constructed boundary with a defensive function.

## Notes

Distinct from fe-no-ka (W056, forbidden boundary): fe-su is a constructed barrier that holds; fe-no-ka is a prohibition that defines a boundary. A ward is built; a taboo is declared. Also distinct from ha-fe (thermal threshold, S034) and si-fe (signal threshold, S067): those are measurement boundaries, not structural ones.

## Related
