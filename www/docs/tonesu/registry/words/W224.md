## Composition

## Definition

the deliberate act of corrupting or degrading a signal to mislead a recipient; strategic deception; misdirection. Default right-branching parse: ka modifies [si-de], where si-de = signal-decrease = the reduction of signal fidelity. Ka-si-de = intentional [signal-decrease] = deliberate degradation of information fidelity for strategic purposes. Distinguished from simple ignorance (absence of knowledge) by the ka (intentionality) component: ka-si-de is always an agentive act, not an accident or omission.


## Notes

First attestation AOW-001-A (S702). Sun Tzu's foundational axiom "all warfare is based on deception" is rendered as a-ka-ra ne go-si ka-si-de. The three-root compound carries ka (intentionality), si (information/signal), and de (decrease/corruption): deception as intentional information degradation, not mere falsehood-utterance. Consistent with an information-theoretic analysis of deception: lying is a signal manipulation that reduces the recipient's epistemic access to states of the world.
