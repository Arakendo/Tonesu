## Composition

ka (deliberate action) + mu (artifact) — artifact whose identity is the action it performs. Head-final: mu (artifact) is head; ka specifies functional class.

## Definition

tool; an artifact defined by its action-function; an instrument for performing a specific deliberate action.

## Notes

Distinct from ra-ki-mu (engine/power-machine, W012) where the artifact is defined by energy and motion. ka-mu is the generic functional tool: the artifact is defined by what deliberate action it affords. Used in S047A (ka-mu-to = concept of device-maintenance) and S071 (ka-mu as physical object). Also appears as the suffix exemplar in morphology.md (-mu = device/instrument; ka-mu → tool for the action).

## Related

mu (artifact), ka (deliberate action), ra-ki-mu (W012), ko-mu (W052, vessel)

## Examples

"la-ze  lo-ka-mu  ne-ko-mu  ka-ko" — he placed the tool into the container (S071)
