## Composition

ka (deliberate action / intentionality) + ra (force/energy). Head-final: ra (force) is head; ka specifies the force as deliberate and intentional rather than natural or incidental. 2-root compound.

## Definition

deliberate application of force; combat; martial action; warfare as an activity. The intersection of intentional action (ka) and energy/force (ra): an agent's deliberate direction of force against another. Distinct from ra-ki (W038: storm — non-volitional force-in-motion) by the ka modifier: ka-ra is always willed, targeted, and agentive.


## Notes

First attestation AOW-001 (S703). Core vocabulary for the Art of War stress test. Distinct from ra alone (raw force, non-volitional), ra-ki (W038, storm/force-in-motion), and ka alone (deliberate action without force specification). a-ka-ra (a-prefix + ka-ra) = universal-scoped warfare, all instances of warfare, used as topic NP in S702. The ne go-si ka-ra construction (not-by-means-of-combat) appears in S704 as a manner complement.
