## Composition

si (signal/representation) + ki (motion) — the motion of a signal toward a recipient

## Definition

transmit; send a signal or message; directed point-to-point signal motion

## Notes

Distinct from W021 si-ne-ki (communication/networking): si-ki is directed one-way transmission (signal + motion); si-ne-ki is relational communication (signal + relation + motion), implying a maintained connection or broadcast.
Use si-ki for "send"; si-ne-ki for "communicate / network."

## Related

si-ne-ki (W021), si-ki-mu (transmitter / sender device)

## Examples

"la-ze  lo-si  ka-si-ki" — she sent the message (S017)
