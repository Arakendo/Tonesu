## Notes

W189. First attested DND-002 (S612, Chaotic Good Ranger). no-su (chaos/ no-structure) is an established unit in the registry (no-su'ma W115). wi-no-su extends this to the moral/dispositional sphere: a person whose will is organized around the ABSENCE of structural constraint. Three-root compound; the middle root no is a negation prefix, making the overall structure wi-(no-(su)) but with right-branching defaulting to wi-(no-su) as a unit.
