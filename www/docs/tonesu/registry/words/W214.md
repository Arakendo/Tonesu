## Composition

ko (containment) + ha (heat/thermal state) + mu (device/artifact) = containment-[heat-device] = the device that contains heat for thermal processing.
Right-branch parse: ko-[ha-mu] = containment-[thermal device].
Distinct from ra-ki-mu (W012, engine = energy-motion-device): an engine converts energy to motion; an oven holds thermal state steady for transformation.

## Definition

oven; thermal containment device. The device defined by containing heat for the purpose of cooking or thermally transforming materials placed inside it. `ko-` (containment) provides the structural frame that makes it an oven rather than an open flame; `ha-mu` (heat-device) is the device-type. Covers all oven types: domestic baking oven, industrial kiln, ceramic kiln, bread oven.
