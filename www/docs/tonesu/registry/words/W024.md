## Composition

fe (boundary/limit) + si (signal) — head-final: a signal characterized as boundary-marking; a signal that a limit is approaching, present, or has been crossed

## Definition

warn; issue a warning or alert; a signal that marks a boundary or limit condition

## Notes

fe (boundary) characterizes warnings precisely: a warning signals that a limit exists, is approaching, or has been crossed. Preferred over de-si (decay-signal), which conflates the warning with the harm itself. head-final: si is the head (it is a signal); fe specifies what kind.

## Related

fe-si-mu (warning device / alarm), fe-ki (to reach a limit / become bounded)

## Examples

"ka-fe-si  ne-yu" — warn them / issue a warning to them "la-ze  lo-si  ka-si-ki-past  wi [ka-fe-si  ne-yu]" — she sent the message to warn them (S017)
