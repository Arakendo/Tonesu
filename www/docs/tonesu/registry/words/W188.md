## Composition

wi (will) + su (structure). Head su: the will IS organized by structure. Two-root compound; no additional specification needed — lawfulness is will-plus-structure with no further constraint.

## Notes

W188. First attested DND-002 (S609 framing, S610 Lawful Good). Paired with wi-no-su (W189, chaotic) and wi-su-fe (W192, law-neutral) to cover the full law/chaos axis. Cross-batch: the Jedi Code (STW-001) and Sith Code (STW-002) are both wi-su in character — their codes are structured causal chains — differing only on the wi-vo/wi-de (ethics) axis. Distinct from wi-re (W099, feedback loop), wi-du (W175, victory), wi-fe (W100, rule). Do not confuse with wi-fe: wi-su is a PERSON'S will-quality; wi-fe is a normative OBJECT (a rule/claim).

## Related
