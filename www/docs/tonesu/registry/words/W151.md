## Composition

ra (energy/force) + lu (light/visibility) + ki (motion/process) = energy-light-in-motion = fire as process.

## Definition

fire; the energetic-luminous process; energy emitting light while in motion. Physical fire as a natural process. Distinct from ra (energy alone) or lu (light alone): fire is specifically the coupled process of energy releasing light through motion/change.

## Notes

Used both as noun compound (the fire) and process predicate (was burning / fire-process happening). S402: la-zo-su  ra-lu-ki no  de = the plant fire-processed but did not decrease = the burning bush miracle.
Related compound: no-de'ra-lu-ki (fire-without-decrease) is a CLQ-EXT candidate — the miracle as a lexical chunk. Not registered yet; awaiting CLQ-EXT rule resolution.
