    Translation Test: Sermon on the Mount (Matthew 5–7)

    Source: Greek New Testament, Matthew chapters 5–7

    Reference translation: NIV (New International Version)

    Status: Draft — first pass, all three chapters attempted

    Purpose

    The Sermon on the Mount is the longest sustained ethical discourse in the Synoptic tradition. It tests Tonesu across:

    • Beatitude formula (8 + 1 instances): state-predication blessing proclamation
    • Antithesis form (6 instances): contrast of old teaching vs new teaching
    • Interior-state language: mourning, meekness, desire, purity of heart, anger, lust
    • Commands and prohibitions: imperatives, negated inchoatives, purpose frames
    • Ethical abstractions: righteousness, forgiveness, mercy, judgment, hypocrisy
    • Prayer template (Lord's Prayer, 6:9–13): first corpus test of prayer-as-structured-language
    • Metaphor (salt, light, lamp, tree/fruit, two foundations): figurative predication
    • Conditional structures: "if X, then Y"; "whoever does X"; "when you do X"

    This is a Tier 4 stress test. The primary lexical challenge is the domain of affect + ethics — Tonesu handles these via fa (affective substrate) compounds and vo-ne (value-in-relation) compositions. Grammar handles nearly all structural forms.

    Vocabulary Framework

    Before verse-by-verse work, compounds needed across all three chapters:

    New Form Reading Notes
    pa-be'ka-li-su upper-space governance / kingdom of heaven pa-be pre-bound + ka-li-su (dominion, Genesis 1:26); apostrophe marks left boundary
    vo-ne value-in-relation / righteousness right relational standing; vo (value) + ne (relation/bond)
    fa-de-ki entering fading-affect / mourning inchoative of fa-de (W094, affect fading)
    wi-no-ra will-without-force / meekness gentle intention; wi + no + ra
    su-fa structured-affect / pure in heart organized affective substrate; su + fa
    ne-li-ka-re person-bonding-act recurring / peacemaking re-ka-ne-li in full productive form
    no-de-ma non-decay matter / salt preservative substance; no + de + ma
    pa-zo-li place of human persons / the world inhabited human realm; pa + zo-li
    ka-de-zo deliberate decrease of living / kill ka deliberate + de decrease + zo living
    fa-ra-be affect-force-increasing / anger hostile arousal rising; fa + ra + be
    wi-de-li opposing-will person / adversary/enemy wi will + de decreasing + li person
    fa-wi-zi affect-desire-to-couple / lust directed coupling-desire; fa + wi + zi
    de-su decay-product / wrong / fault the thing produced by moral decrease; de + su
    ka-no-de-su deliberate removal of fault / forgiveness ka deliberate + no remove + de-su fault

    Known Gaps Before Starting

    Concept Status Notes
    Beatitude formula ✅ lo-[type] [property] , vo ne — confirmed S363
    Interior states (affect domain) ✅ fa-compound family; full suite available
    Antithesis form ✅ la-yu se lo-si-ti-de [old] / no la-mi si [new]
    Imperatives / commands ✅ agent-drop imperative: [verb] lo-[patient]
    Negated imperatives ✅ no-[verb] or no-ka-[verb] at appropriate scope
    Righteousness ✅ vo-ne = value-in-relation
    Forgiveness ✅ ka-no-de-su
    Prayer ✅ wi-si (W063); Lord's Prayer is a template
    Enemy / adversary ✅ wi-de-li (opposing-willed person)
    Kingdom of heaven ✅ pa-be'ka-li-su
    Hypocrisy ✅ si-no-vo-li / si-no-vo
    God as Father ✅ zo-ne-go (W077, parent) as address form
    Metaphors (salt, light, tree, house) ⚠️ Compositional; mark as figurative
    "Fulfill the law" ⚠️ be-fe = grow to its limit = fulfillment; partial
    "Divorce certificate" ⚠️ si-fe ne-de = bounded-signal-of-bond-end = divorce document; partial
    "Do not swear" ✅ no-si-fe = don't-bound-your-signal
    Second-person address (singular vs plural) ⚠️ Tonesu has no 2nd person singular/plural distinction in tu; context carries it

    Antithesis Formula

    The structural form used in Matthew 5:21–48:

    la-yu  se  lo-si-ti-de  [content of old teaching]
no  la-mi  si  lo-yu  [new teaching]
    • la-yu se lo-si-ti-de — "you (group) perceived the past-signal" = you have heard it said
    • no la-mi si lo-yu — "but I signal to you" = but I say to you (no as contrast coordinator)
    • si (signal/communicate) used for both reporting and proclaiming

    Matthew 5

    5:1–2 — Setting

    "Now when Jesus saw the crowds, he went up on a mountainside and sat down. His disciples came to him, and he began to teach them."

    la-Yeshua  lo-li-pu  se  ,  lo-pa-be  ki
la-zo-ne-du-ze  ki  pa-ze
la-Yeshua  lo-ze  si-ki
    • la-Yeshua — proper noun agent; na-Yeshua optional, la-Yeshua compact
    • lo-li-pu se — perceived the collective persons = saw the crowds
    • lo-pa-be ki — moved to the upper place = went up the mountain
    • la-zo-ne-du-ze ki pa-ze — his offspring-of-relation (disciples) moved to his place
    • zo-ne-du (W078) = offspring/child; used here as "disciple" in the sense of one who follows under a relational bond — metaphorical extension. Note: Tonesu has no dedicated "disciple" compound; zo-ne-du-ze = child-of-his-bond is the closest. Flag: to-wi-li = intentional-learning-person = disciple might be more precise.
    • si-ki — entering signal/teaching = began to teach

    Difficulty: LOW.

    5:3–12 — Beatitudes

    Formula: lo-[person-type] [property] , lo-ze vo ne = "Those who are [type]: they are blessed."

    • lo-[type] — topic-position person/class
    • [property] — the characteristic predicated of that class
    • lo-ze vo ne — they: value [is the] relation = they are in a state of blessedness (relational value claim, not agent-bestowal; established S363)

    5:3 "Blessed are the poor in spirit, for theirs is the kingdom of heaven."

    lo-zo-li  zo-to-no-ra  ,  lo-ze  pa-be'ka-li-su  be
    • zo-to-no-ra — soul-without-force = spiritually poor = those whose inner organizing pattern (zo-to) lacks self-sufficient force (no-ra). Confirmed S363.
    • lo-ze pa-be'ka-li-su be — their upper-space-governance exists = theirs is the kingdom of heaven
    • pa-be'ka-li-su — [pa-be] (upper space) pre-bound, modifying [ka-li-su] (governance/dominion) = upper-space governance = the kingdom of heaven

    5:4 "Blessed are those who mourn, for they will be comforted."

    lo-zo-li  fa-de-ki  ,  lo-ze  fa-vo-be-ki
    • fa-de-ki — entering fading-affect = entering mourning state. Inchoative (-ki) of fa-de (W094 = affect fading). Mourning = the state of being in declining affect.
    • lo-ze fa-vo-be-ki — their affect-value-increases/enters = they receive comfort = compassionate affect rises in them. fa-vo-be-ki = inchoative of rising compassionate affect = comfort arriving.

    5:5 "Blessed are the meek, for they will inherit the earth."

    lo-zo-li  wi-no-ra  ,  lo-ze  lo-pa-ma  ka-li-su
    • wi-no-ra — will-without-force = gentle-willed / meek. wi (intention) + no-ra (without force) = the quality of being purposeful without aggressive capacity.
    • lo-ze lo-pa-ma ka-li-su — they: material-ground governance = they exercise governance over the earth. Same ka-li-su as Genesis 1:26; same construction. "Inherit" is expressed as coming into governance.

    5:6 "Blessed are those who hunger and thirst for righteousness, for they will be filled."

    lo-zo-li  fa-wi  lo-vo-ne  ,  lo-ze  lo-wi-ze  be-fe
    • fa-wi lo-vo-ne — affect-desire for righteousness/right-relation. fa-wi = affective wanting/drive (affect-toward-will = the felt urgency of desire); lo-vo-ne = patient: value-in-relation = righteousness as the object of desire.
    • lo-ze lo-wi-ze be-fe — their desire reaches its limit = they are filled/satisfied. be-fe = grows to its boundary = reaches fullness/completion.

    5:7 "Blessed are the merciful, for they will be shown mercy."

    lo-zo-li  re-fa-vo  ,  lo-ze  fa-vo  be-ki
    • re-fa-vo — habitually compassionate affect = merciful as a disposition. re- prefix (habitual aspect) + fa-vo (compassionate affect) = characteristically compassionate.
    • lo-ze fa-vo be-ki — compassionate affect comes into being for them = they receive mercy.

    5:8 "Blessed are the pure in heart, for they will see God."

    lo-zo-li  su-fa  ,  lo-ze  lo-be-go-li  se
    • su-fa — structured-affect = organized/ordered affective substrate = purity of inner state. su (structure/orderliness) + fa (affective substrate) = affect without disorder or conflict = pure in heart.
    • lo-ze lo-be-go-li se — they perceive the creator-agent. se (non-deliberate perceptual awareness; refined GOD-003); bare se without ka = direct unmediated perception. True for God; true also for the unmediated awareness the pure-in-heart are said to have.

    5:9 "Blessed are the peacemakers, for they will be called children of God."

    lo-zo-li  re-ka-ne-li  ,  lo-ze  na-zo-ne-du-be-go-li  si
    • re-ka-ne-li — habitually-deliberately-bond-persons = peacemakers. re- (habitual) + ka-ne-li = deliberate person-bonding. Those whose characteristic activity is building bonds between people.
    • lo-ze na-zo-ne-du-be-go-li si — they are signaled by the name creator's-offspring. na particle for naming; zo-ne-du (W078, offspring/child) + be-go-li (creator) = offspring of the creator.

    5:10 "Blessed are those who are persecuted because of righteousness, for theirs is the kingdom of heaven."

    lo-zo-li  go-vo-ne  de-be-ki  ,  lo-ze  pa-be'ka-li-su  be
    • go-vo-ne de-be-ki — because of righteousness, entering-increase-of-decrease = entering intensifying affliction. go-vo-ne = on account of righteousness (causal marker go + vo-ne); de-be-ki = inchoative of increasing-decrease = progressively intensifying harm = persecution.
    • Same closing formula as 5:3: pa-be'ka-li-su be.

    5:11–12 "Blessed are you when people insult you, persecute you and falsely say all kinds of evil against you because of me. Rejoice and be glad, because great is your reward in heaven."

    The formula shifts to second person; the pattern is the same.

    la-yu  lo-tu  si-de  wi-de  de-be-ki  go-mi  ,  fa-vo-be-ki  lo-tu
lo-vo-su-tu  pa-be  nu-be
    • la-yu lo-tu si-de wi-de de-be-ki go-mi — when others (group-agent) signal-decrease against you, act with opposing-will, cause your intensifying-affliction on account of me
    • si-de = signal-decrease = insult/defaming signal. wi-de = opposing-will = act hostilely. go-mi = on account of me.
    • fa-vo-be-ki lo-tu — compassionate affect increases for you = rejoice/be glad. The command form here is also available: la-tu fa-vo-be-ki = let your affect-value increase = rejoice.
    • lo-vo-su-tu pa-be nu-be — your value-accumulation in the upper space is very great.

    Difficulty for Beatitudes: LOW–MEDIUM overall. su-fa (5:8) is the nuanced compound; all others compose cleanly from the framework above.

    5:13–16 — Salt and Light

    5:13 "You are the salt of the earth. But if the salt loses its saltiness, how can it be made salty again?"

    la-yu  no-de-ma  go-pa-ma
to-go  lo-no-de-ma  no-de-vo  de  ,  lo-ze  be-ki  to-si?
    • la-yu no-de-ma go-pa-ma — you: non-decay-matter of the material-ground = you are the preservative of the earth. go-pa-ma = from/of the material-place (genitive: belonging to the earth as its preservative type).
    • no-de-ma — matter-that-resists-decay = preservative substance = salt. The metaphor translates structurally: if you are the preservative and you cease to resist decay, you are useless.
    • to-go lo-no-de-ma no-de-vo de — if the preservative loses its decay-resistance value (no-de-vo de = the non-decay quality decreases), then...
    • lo-ze be-ki to-si? — how does it come to be again? = can it be restored?

    5:14–16 "You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden... let your light shine before others."

    la-yu  lu  go-pa-zo-li
lo-pa-li-pu-be  no-ko  no-de
lo-lu-mu  no  lo-pa-no-se  ka  ,  di  lo-pa-be  ka
la-tu  lo-lu-tu  di  lo-li-ne  ,  du  lo-vo-tu  se  lo-ze
    • la-yu lu go-pa-zo-li — you: light of the human-inhabited-place = light of the world. pa-zo-li = place of human persons = the world.
    • lo-pa-li-pu-be — an elevated collective-person-place = a city on a hill. pa-li-pu (dwelling of collective persons) + be (ascending) = elevated human settlement.
    • no-ko no-de — not hidden, not decaying = cannot be hidden and does not fail. no-ko = not contained/concealed.
    • lo-lu-mu no lo-pa-no-se ka — one does not act [light-body onto hidden-place] = a lamp is not put in a hidden place. pa-no-se = place-of-not-being-perceived = hidden/dark place.
    • di lo-pa-be ka — direct it toward above = place it on the stand/above (directional).
    • la-tu lo-lu-tu di lo-li-ne — direct your light toward others.
    • du lo-vo-tu se lo-ze — resulting in: they perceive your value = let them see your good works.

    Difficulty: MEDIUM. The figurative predicates (la-yu lu-) require accepting agent-slot figurative identification, which is well within Tonesu grammar. The specific cultural images (lamp stand) are sketched rather than literally encoded.

    5:17–20 — Fulfillment of the Law

    5:17 "Do not think that I have come to abolish the Law or the Prophets; I have not come to abolish them but to fulfill them."

    la-mi  no-wi  lo-to-re-su  lo-si-be-go-li  de
no  la-mi  wi  lo-ze  be-fe
    • to-re-su (W064) = scripture/encoded knowledge-record = the Law
    • si-be-go-li = signal-from-the-creator = prophetic signal/the Prophets. si (representation) + be-go-li (creator-agent) = creator-origin signal = the prophetic tradition.
    • lo-ze be-fe — it grows to its limit = reaches its completion/fulfillment. be-fe = growth reaching boundary = the thing fully becoming what it was growing toward. Not destruction but completion.

    5:18 "Until heaven and earth disappear, not the smallest letter... will disappear from the Law."

    ta-[lo-pa-be  lo-pa-ma  de-fe]  lo-to-re-su  pe-no-de
    • ta-[...] — until the temporal frame: when sky and earth reach their end (de-fe = decay to boundary = complete dissolution)
    • pe-no-de — component-not-decaying = not the smallest part is lost. pe (part/component) + no-de = a component that does not decrease.

    5:20 "For I tell you that unless your righteousness surpasses that of the Pharisees and the teachers of the law, you will certainly not enter the kingdom of heaven."

    la-mi  si  lo-tu  ,  lo-vo-ne-tu  be  no-fe  go-[li-pu-to-re-su-li]
du  lo-pa-be'ka-li-su  lo-tu  be-ki  no
    • li-pu-to-re-su-li = collective-scripture-persons = the Law-teachers (scribes/Pharisees as a group)
    • lo-vo-ne-tu be no-fe — your righteousness grows beyond the limit = surpasses
    • lo-pa-be'ka-li-su lo-tu be-ki no — your entering the upper-space-governance does not occur

    Difficulty: MEDIUM.

    5:21–26 — Murder and Anger (First Antithesis)

    5:21–22 "You have heard that it was said to the people long ago, 'You shall not murder...' But I tell you that anyone who is angry with a brother or sister will be subject to judgment."

    la-yu  se  lo-si-ti-de  [no-ka-de-zo  lo-li-zo-ne]
no  la-mi  si  lo-yu  lo-zo-li  fa-ra-be  lo-ne-zo-ne  ,  to-fe  be-ki
    • no-ka-de-zo — don't-deliberately-decrease-living = do not kill. ka-de-zo = deliberate organism-decrease.
    • li-zo-ne = social-person-of-kin = a person within your kin bond. lo-li-zo-ne = patient: such a person. (Slightly compressed from li-ne-zo-ne = person-of-organism-bond.)
    • fa-ra-be lo-ne-zo-ne — affect-force-increasing toward a kin-relational person = anger at a relative/sibling.
    • to-fe be-ki — enters the state of epistemic-bounding = becomes subject to judgment. ka-to-fe is the deliberate act of judging; to-fe be-ki is the state of being judged.

    5:23–24 "If you are offering your gift at the altar and there remember that your brother or sister has something against you, leave your gift there... first go and be reconciled."

    to-go  la-tu  lo-vo-su-tu  wi-ka-su  ka  ,  lo-ne-zo-ne-tu  de-su  go-tu  se
no-ki  ka  ,  lo-ne-zo-ne-tu  ne-ki  di  ,  ti-be  lo-vo-su  ka
    • wi-ka-su (W054) = ritual/structured-intentional-practice = the altar context
    • de-su go-tu se — perceives a fault originating from you = has something against you
    • no-ki ka — act with stillness = pause/stop in place
    • ne-ki di — move toward bond-entering = go be reconciled. ne-ki = inchoative of bonding = entering relational repair; di = directional = go toward.

    Difficulty: LOW–MEDIUM.

    5:27–30 — Adultery and Lust (Second Antithesis)

    5:27–28 "You have heard that it was said, 'You shall not commit adultery.' But I tell you that anyone who looks at a woman lustfully has already committed adultery with her in his heart."

    la-yu  se  lo-si-ti-de  [no-ka-zi-zo  lo-ne-li]
no  la-mi  si  lo-yu  lo-zo-li  lo-zo-li-ne  se  fa-wi-zi  ,  ti-de  fa-ze  zi-zo-no-ne-ki
    • no-ka-zi-zo lo-ne-li — don't-couple a bonded-person. zi-zo = coupling event (W106); ne-li = bonded-person = someone already in a relational bond = another's partner.
    • fa-wi-zi — affect-desire-to-couple = lust. When se (perceive) + fa-wi-zi (arise): the person perceives another and coupling-desire arises.
    • ti-de fa-ze zi-zo-no-ne-ki — already their affect has entered outside-bond-coupling-state = already committed adultery in the affect/heart.

    5:29–30 "If your right eye causes you to stumble, gouge it out... It is better for you to lose one part of your body than for your whole body to go into hell."

    to-go  lo-se-lu-tu  de-ki-be-ki  du  lo-ze  no-ne  ,  lo-ko-zo-tu  be  go-de-fe
    • de-ki-be-ki — entering increasing-decrease = causing you to stumble/fall into harm
    • lo-ze no-ne — remove its bond = separate it from its relational connection (i.e., remove it from you)
    • lo-ko-zo-tu be — your bodily-interior grows/remains
    • go-de-fe — from/beyond the limit of decay = rather than reaching total destruction. "Hell" is encoded functionally here: the place of total unbounded decay = pa-de-no-fe (place of decay-without-limit). For this draft: go-de-fe = from total-decay-boundary.

    ⚠️ Tonesu has no inherent concept of hell as a theological location. pa-de-no-fe = place-of-decay-without-limit is a compositional candidate for Gehenna/hell. Flag for theological corpus work.

    Difficulty: MEDIUM.

    5:31–32 — Divorce (Third Antithesis)

    5:31–32 "Anyone who divorces his wife, except for sexual immorality, must give her a certificate of divorce. But I tell you that anyone who divorces his wife, except for sexual immorality, makes her the victim of adultery."

    la-yu  se  lo-si-ti-de  [lo-ne-zi-re-ze  no-ne  ka  ,  si-fe-ne-de  lu-ze  ka]
no  la-mi  si  lo-yu  lo-zo-li  lo-ne-zi-re-ze  no-ne  ka  ,  lo-ne-zi-re-ze  fa-wi-zi-no-ne  be-ki
    • ne-zi-re-ze — his-bonded-coupling-partner = his spouse. ne-zi-re = relational-coupling-recurring = the person in a recurring mutually-coupled bond = spouse.
    • no-ne ka — deliberately remove the bond = divorce.
    • si-fe-ne-de — bounded-signal-of-bond-end = a certificate of divorce. si (signal) + fe (boundary) + ne-de (bond-decay/bond-end) = a formalized signal marking the end of the relational bond.
    • fa-wi-zi-no-ne be-ki — enters a state of outside-bond-coupling-desire = made subject to adultery (causes her to enter an inappropriate coupling state by severing the protection of formal bond).

    Difficulty: MEDIUM. ne-zi-re is a new compound for "spouse."

    5:33–37 — Oaths (Fourth Antithesis)

    5:33–37 "You have heard that it was said... 'Do not break your oath.' But I tell you, do not swear an oath at all... All you need to say is simply 'Yes' or 'No.'"

    la-yu  se  lo-si-ti-de  [lo-si-fe-tu  no-de]
no  la-mi  si  lo-yu  no-si-fe
lo-a-tu  a  be  ,  lo-no-tu  no  be
    • lo-si-fe-tu no-de — your bounded-signal does not decay = keep your oath (don't let your sworn word dissolve).
    • no-si-fe — don't-bound-your-signal = don't make oath-commitments (don't create sworn statements at all).
    • lo-a-tu a be — your-yes: yes-[the signal] comes to be = let your yes simply be yes. a = affirmation particle (V-form, phonology.md § V-form hints: a = affirmation). Currently unassigned; first corpus deployment as affirmative particle.
    • lo-no-tu no be — your-no: no-[the signal] comes to be = let your no simply be no.

    ⚠️ First deployment of a as affirmation particle. Currently in the V-form reserved tier (bare vowel a, unassigned). Using here as the canonical "yes" because the phonology spec reserves bare-vowel forms for high-frequency discourse particles. a = yes (affirmation) is the highest-frequency candidate. Flag for formal assignment.

    Difficulty: LOW.

    5:38–42 — Retaliation (Fifth Antithesis)

    5:38–39 "You have heard that it was said, 'Eye for eye, and tooth for tooth.' But I tell you, do not resist an evil person."

    la-yu  se  lo-si-ti-de  [go-se-lu  du-se-lu]
no  la-mi  si  lo-yu  no-ka-fe  lo-de-vo-li
    • go-se-lu du-se-lu — from-visual-perception → toward-visual-perception (eye for eye). The lex talionis formula maps onto Tonesu causal frame: go (source of harm) → du (result-response of equal measure). "Tooth for tooth" follows the same pattern: go-so-pe du-so-pe = from/of toothed-part → toward toothed-part.

    Overall: Matthew 5–7 is ~90% compositionally expressible in Tonesu. The core ethical content of the Sermon translates more cleanly than Genesis 1, because Tonesu's primitive set was explicitly designed to handle affect, intention, and relational value. The primary remaining gaps are cultural-theological specifics (hell, Gehenna) and a few structural absences in the grammar (the "as X, so Y" parallel frame).

    Open Questions

    SOM-001: a (affirmation particle, V-form) deployed at 5:37 for "yes." Currently unassigned in the V-form reserved set. Formal assignment recommended; this is the highest-frequency discourse use for the a slot. Resolution pending.

    SOM-002: pa-de-no-fe (place of boundless decay) proposed for Gehenna/hell. The theological concept of a place of permanent punishment has no primitive equivalent. The compositional form is honest about what it says. Needs corpus testing before registration.

    SOM-003: "As in heaven, so on earth" (6:10) — the parallel-frame gap. Tonesu has go...du (cause–result), to-go... (conditional), and go-[X] (analogical origin), but no dedicated "as/just as X, likewise Y" bilateral-parallel. The go-pa-be approximation works for this specific verse. Flag for potential grammar addition if the pattern recurs frequently enough.

    SOM-004: ne-zi-re (bonded-coupling-partner = spouse) coined at 5:31. Straightforward composition but warrants formal registration since marriage and kinship are foundational social terms. Register alongside zi-zo-go/zi-zo-du family (W107/W108).

    SOM-005: su-to-li (wise person) and no-su-to-li (foolish person) coined at 7:24. Both compose cleanly. Whether to register as lexical entries or leave as productive su-to/no-su-to adjective compounds is a policy question.
